Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu since Fall 2016. Previously, I received my bachelor degree in Microelectronics from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) in June, 2016.

My research interests include design for manufacturing, physical design and machine learning on chips.
