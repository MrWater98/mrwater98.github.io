Biography

I'm a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu since Fall 2016. Previously, I received my bachelor degree in Microelectronics from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) in June, 2016.

My research interests include machine learning on chips, design for manufacturing and physical design.
